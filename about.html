Massimo Vendola

Freelance Photographer/Videographer/VFX Artist

Born, raised, and living in Brooklyn, photography has always been a passion for me. Over the years I have expanded to videography, visual effects, and CGI, allowing myself to further develop my artistic expressions. Using programs such as After Effects, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, and Blender, I am able to make art of the real world, through my own lens. In the college I attend, Brooklyn College, my work has been published in several publication events and school newspapers. Outside of my craft, I am an aspiring Computer Science Major, expecting to graduate in May 2021.
